You cannot use this service to request a certificate for a:

  • privately funded apprenticeship
  • framework based apprenticeship

Before you start

You need:

  • permission from the apprentice for you to apply for a certificate on their behalf
  • the unique learner number (ULN) for the apprentice
  • an Information Management Services (IDAMS) account
