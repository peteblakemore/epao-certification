We’re pleased to let you know we’ve launched our new online service for recording apprentice end-point assessment grades and requesting certificates. We invite you to register now and use the new service the next time you’re recording grades and requesting a certificate.

Please follow the steps below to get started.

Step 1 - Request access to the ESFA’s Information Management Services (IDAMS) system

The Information Management Services system is a secure way of accessing ESFA digital services, including Record Apprentice End-Point Assessment Grades.

The first person that sets up an account will automatically be assigned as the ‘super user’. That means they have admin rights and can give access to other members of their organisation once they've set up their account. You can change the superuser whenever you need to.

Request access to the Information Management Services system.

When prompted, enter your organisation’s unique UKPRN: xxxxxxxxx

Step 2 - Request the end-point assessment organisations (EPAO) role permissions

Once your access is approved, sign in to your account.

On the next screen, click ‘My identity’

Select ‘Add a new permission’ link

Add ‘End Point Assessment’, which is on the second page

Step 3 - Record apprentice end-point assessment grades

Access the new Record apprentice end-point assessment grades service.

Sign in with your email address and password you created in step 1.
