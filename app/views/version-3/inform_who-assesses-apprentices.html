Who assesses apprentices

Assessments must be carried out by a government-approved End-Point Assessment Organisation (EPAO). [HYPERLINK TO REGISTER OF END-POINT ASSESSMENT ORGANISATIONS]

The EPAO:

  • conducts a full and independent end-point assessment of an apprenticeâ€™s skills and capabilities

  • adheres to quality assurance requirements
  • informs the Education and Skills Funding Agency (ESFA) when an apprentice has passed their end-point assessment so that the ESFA can send out apprenticeship certificates
