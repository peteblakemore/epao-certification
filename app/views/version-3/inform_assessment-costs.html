Assessment costs

The cost of each EPA will vary according to the requirements set out in the standards â€“ such as assessment tools, methods and estimated completion times. However, the EPA is capped at a maximum of 20% of the overall cost of the apprenticeship delivery.

For non-levy payers, the employer will pay one third of the EPA costs and the SFA will pay two thirds. For levy-paying employers, the EPA will be paid for by their levy contributions.
