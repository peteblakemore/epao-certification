Does the end-point assessment count towards qualifications?

No. The EPA is separate to any qualifications or other assessment that the apprentice may undertake during training.

Assessments will either be conducted by an independent third party or in such a way that no party who has been involved in the apprenticeship can make the sole decision on competence and passing the EPA
