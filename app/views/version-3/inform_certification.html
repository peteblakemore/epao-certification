Certification

An apprenticeship certificate provides formal recognition that an individual has achieved their apprenticeship.

Responsibility for processing an apprentice’s certificate depends on the type of apprenticeship they have completed:

  • frameworks – apprentices and training providers can apply for a certificate from Apprenticeship Certificates England
  • standards – end-point assessment organisations must apply for certificates on behalf of apprentices through the [NAME TBC] service
